Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide sports enthusiasts with the platform to connect to sports teams in order to play at a time and a place that suits them.
Our app/website will allow players to take part in their favourite hobby, make exercise more fun and competitive, and furthermore help inspire our users find a new passion for sport.
